# Medical & 3D Body Imaging Dataset Research

## Summary for BZ

### UK Biobank (Requires Application)
- **Size:** 500,000+ participants
- **Imaging:** MRI (brain, heart, abdomen), DXA/DEXA (bone density/body composition), carotid ultrasound
- **Access:** Requires formal application, ~3-6 month approval
- **Cost:** Free for research, but application needed
- **Value:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Best real medical imaging dataset
- **Action:** Submit application at ukbiobank.ac.uk/enable-your-research

### CAESAR Dataset
- **Size:** 4,431 US/European civilians
- **Data:** 3D body scans + measurements
- **Cost:** ~$10,000+ (commercial)
- **Access:** Purchase through SAE International
- **Value:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Gold standard anthropometry, but expensive

### SMPL Body Model (FREE)
- **Source:** Max Planck Institute
- **Data:** 3D parametric body model (mesh topology)
- **Cost:** FREE with academic license
- **Access:** smpl.is.tue.mpg.de
- **Value:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Essential for 3D body modeling
- **Action:** Can download immediately

### UP-3D Dataset (FREE)
- **Size:** 26,239 images with SMPL parameters
- **Source:** Merged from LSP, MPII, FashionPose
- **Cost:** FREE
- **Access:** research-data.tsinghua.edu.cn
- **Value:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Images + 3D annotations

### PeopleSnapshot (FREE)
- **Size:** 24 subjects
- **Data:** RGB video of people rotating
- **Source:** MPI
- **Value:** ⭐⭐⭐ Small but high quality

### HumanDataset.com
- **Data:** 3D human scans
- **Service:** Will register scans to SMPL
- **Cost:** Contact for pricing
- **Value:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Custom 3D scanning service
